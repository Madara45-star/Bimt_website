Membership in prestigious professional bodies further showcases Mr. Farshath's commitment to continuous learning and professional development. These affiliations reflect his dedication to maintaining high standards in his field.

Notably, Mr. Farshath's passion for teaching has earned him a reputation as a smart and highly effective lecturer. He is frequently invited by leading schools in Colombo to teach advanced subjects, a testament to his expertise and ability to inspire students. An innovative and knowledgeable professional, Mr. Farshath has consistently delivered outstanding results throughout his career. His leadership skills, combined with his expertise in education and finance make him a valuable asset in the industry. As he continues to contribute to the field, his commitment to excellence and dedication to his students and colleagues are sure to lead to even greater achievements.

Dr. Chethana Dabare

Head of Academic Affairs

Dr. Chethana Dabare is a highly qualified and experienced professional with a diverse background in academia and business management. With a PhD in Business Management with a specialization in Marketing from the esteemed University of Sri Jayewardenepura, she has gained in-depth knowledge and expertise in the field. Dr. Chethana’s educational journey began with a Master's degree in Business Management in Marketing (MBM) from the University of Kelaniya. She excelled in her studies and received a bachelor's degree in management marketing (B. B. Mgt) with First Class Honors from the same university.

In addition to her marketing qualifications, Dr. Chethana also holds a Chartered Accountancy from the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (ICASL). This qualification has laid a solid foundation of understanding and skills in accounting, auditing, taxation, and general management. Further enhancing her professional skills through continuous learning, Dr. Chethana has obtained a Certificate in Business and Finance from the Institute of Bankers of Sri Lanka (IBSL) and a Chartered Human Resource Management certification from the Institute of Personnel Management Sri Lanka (CIPM). These certifications have broadened her knowledge and skills in finance and human resource management, making her a well-rounded professional.

Dr. Chethana’s career in academia has spanned over a decade, during which she has served as a senior lecturer at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura. In this role, she has been responsible for lecturing, research supervision, and career guidance for students. Additionally, she has served as a higher education quality assurance officer, ensuring that the highest standards of education are maintained in the industry. With her extensive education and experience. She has conducted research, published articles, and presented at conferences, contributing valuable insights in her field of expertise. Dr. Chethana’s passion for academia and her commitment to excellence in education are evident in her achievements and contributions.
