About Our University

A Few Words About the University

BIMT Campus is established in order to offer opportunities to acquire the right knowledge in the area of management and technology, with the collaboration of a prominent international organization. We have aimed to high standard education affordable for the students who are with the hungry of studying while being as one of the tertiary education providers in Sri Lanka.

The Campus is with a strong panel of lecturers who are academically and professionally qualified to share their knowledge and to guide them to succeed in their career. Our lecturers are with the art of adopting modern and structured coaching methods to make the students employable.
