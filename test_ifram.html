Our Journey

  • 2017
    • Established in 2017 to offer opportunities to acquire the right knowledge in the area of management and technology.
    • In the beginning, we offered diploma programs
  • 2018
    • Obtained the Certificate of Incorporation from Registrar of Companies and affiliated with Lincoln University Malaysia to offer degree programmes
  • 2019
    • Became the official country representative of the Institute of Financial Accountants (IFA) in 2019.
  • 2020
    • BIMT Campus has Celebrated its inaugural Graduation Ceremony for 142 BBA in Accounting students.
  • 2021
    • BIMT Campus has Celebrated its second Graduation Ceremony for 372 BBA in Accounting students.
  • 2022
    • International Higher Diploma in Accounting and Finance is Endorsed by Institute of Financial Accountants (IFA).
  • 2023
    • Became ISO 9001: 2015 certified educational organisation.
    • Received Asia Business and Leadership Excellence Award.
    • Signed an agreement with Chartered Management Institute (CMI) to offer various Qualifications in the area of management.
    • Signed the agreement to deliver ATHE Qualifications and engaged in promotional activities.
    • Received Gold Award from the University of Kelaniya.
    • Signed the agreement for collaboration with EDUPRO - UK to over various Qualifications in Level 03 and Level 05.
